About IXAT

We are a team that combined many years of experience of specialists in sphere of taxi services, knowledge and understanding of the market of taxi services from the inside, as well as experience in the development and implementation of the technological base and mobile application interfaces. Based on the current situation in the market of taxi services, namely the beginning of it future robotics, depersonalization of the service provider and the possible monopolization we have identified a separate niche for itself, which is able to compete and to change the current situation in the market of taxi services radically.

Our decision – is Service of independent taxi drivers IXAT!

IXAT - is not only a communication device for independent taxi drivers to passengers, but also a new ideology, the ideology of ordering a taxi by passengers direct to executant. Our service is the next evolutionary step of development in sphere of taxi services.

Our mission – is to bring the taxi market to the independence of taxi services providers, which vying for the customer and, accordingly, improve their quality. Each driver is a small taxi cab company himself, with its own rates, terms and conditions, ratings, reviews and additional services. The most Important in performance of services is a human participation, and it is not only the movement of services from one place to another, but also auxiliary service, the feeling that leaves the process to client.
